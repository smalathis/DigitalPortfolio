Hearty Welcome to Library





The College Library has a very valuable treasure of textbooks,reference books,
and dictionaries, journals (national) magazines and newsletters etc.The college library is established
in theyear 1997. The Library is open to all members of the college.The Library is kept open from
9.15 A.M to 5.00 P.M on all working days, examination days and during summer vacation.
From the academic year 2019­-2020 onwards, every studentis supplied with an Library Card with bar coded
Roll Number. Everystudent can borrow two books at a time from the Library on showing his/her Library
Card Books will be issued only on presentation ofthe Library Card. All students and members of the staff
can access the Internet facility in library during the working hours of all working days.Barcode technology
is used for circulation of books and all the resources have been classified according to Dewey
Decimel Classification scheme(22 ed). The entire Library is maintained with Open Source Koha Library
management software & Ubuntu LTS.
